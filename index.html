GHOST FOREST

Instructions:

>Use the Space key to start playing the game

>Use the Space key to return to the intial state of the game after losing

>Use the Space key to jump while playing the game

Story:

Once a boy named Sanjay entered a dense forest, he was found by the ghost and the ghost wants to kill him. Sanjay is quick so could have easily escaped the forest but there are obstacles which are blocking his path. Thus, help him to quickly escape the forest by helping him to cross the obstacles before the ghost cathes him.
